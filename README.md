Welcome to the CloudKitchen application! Built on the Spring Boot framework, our system revolutionizes the management of cloud kitchen operations. With a comprehensive suite of features, including order and inventory management, role-based access control, and robust reporting capabilities, CloudKitchen streamlines the entire process, from order placement to delivery. Customers can enjoy a seamless ordering experience, while kitchen staff efficiently handle orders and inventory. Administrators gain valuable insights through an intuitive dashboard, ensuring smooth operations and customer satisfaction. Join us in transforming the future of food service with CloudKitchen.
